MEMBERSHIP

Would you like to become a member of the Langwarrin Community Centre?

What does membership mean for you?

  • Allows you to belong to a dynamic organisation which provides much needed services to Langwarrin residents
  • Enables you to contribute in a positive way to the Community in which you live
  • Gives you the right to influence and contribute to the Centre by having the right to vote at Annual General Meetings
  • Allows the opportunity to be elected to the Committee of Management and be directly involved in the governance of the Centre 
  • Lets you receive regular news letters on the Centres activities
